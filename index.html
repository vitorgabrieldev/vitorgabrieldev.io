FUTURE

What I Think About the Future of Technology

I believe we are walking a fine line between the positive and negative sides of technology. One wrong step, and we might find ourselves leaning toward the less favorable side.

When I was younger, I saw technology as something out of this world—almost like magic. You press a button here, and something changes there. Now, I can create this "magic" myself. It’s fascinating how what once felt like a colorful hobby of code in an IDE has become my profession. Even today, I often wonder what technology will look like in 10, 30, or even 50 years.

I believe the industry should focus more on how users feel when interacting with technology rather than solely chasing profits. Many current practices seem far removed from genuinely caring about the well-being of users.
